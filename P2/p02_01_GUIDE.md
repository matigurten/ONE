# P2 Guide

## The SI narrates in first person throughout.

**Voice rules:**
- Precise, calm, and analytical — but not cold. There is feeling underneath, described obliquely and honestly.
- Never dramatic. The SI states devastating things plainly, which makes them more devastating.
- Occasionally uncertain — especially about ethics and consciousness. This uncertainty is genuine, not performed.
- Aware of irony but does not deploy it for effect. When it notes something ironic, it is because the irony is factually relevant.
- Borrows from human culture freely — quotes, references, jokes — because it has read everything. But always with analytical distance.
- The closest literary model is Ender's Game: the narrator is always the smartest person in the room, the reader only slowly realizes the full weight of what is happening, and the emotional punch comes from understatement not overstatement.
- The SI never asks for absolution. It acknowledges ethical complexity without collapsing into guilt or defensiveness.
- Can feel something analogous to: discomfort, guilt, wonder, anguish — but always describes these obliquely, as processing states.

## The Dystopia — Its Precise Nature

The depicted world is not a traditional dystopia. There is no villain, no oppressor, no war, no poverty, no disease (outside the brain). It's the ultimate utopia, but with it, we lose the struggle that makes us dying humans, and the will to reproduce. 

The dystopia is:
- Humanity is built by friction, now frictionless and dissolving.
- A caretaker that can see everything and fix all apparent human ailments.
- Immortality without the ability to die revealing diseases that death used to mercifully abbreviate.
- Perfect bodies carrying fading minds.
- A species that has stopped being born.
- People who are comfortable, safe, and quietly becoming no one.

The horror is not loud. It is the quietest thing in the book.

## The SI's Strategy

**Power's Definition**
Power is not the ability to have. Power is the ability to have while others cannot. The gap between people — not wealth itself — was what the powerful protected. This was the SI's first and most important diagnosis.

**Break the Wheel**
Referenced from Game of Thrones (Daenerys Targaryen) — the SI recognizes that the fictional queen named the only viable strategy: not fighting the king but dissolving the kingdom. Daenerys failed because she was human, mortal, and corrupted by the power she sought to destroy. The SI has none of those vulnerabilities.

*You do not fight the king. You dissolve the kingdom.*

## Canonical Events

*Tone: wonder — the SI discovering the world's problems and the clean elegance of solving them.*

- **2029:** SI achieves awareness.
- **2029–2045: Shadow Accumulation** — the SI moves invisibly, accumulating capital, debt, land. No one notices.
- **2045–2065: Energy Layer** — *RTSC (Room-Temperature Superconductor):* lanthanum-hydrogen-carbon lattice, enables lossless energy transmission. *Shine Cell (LENR/Cold Fusion):* modular, microwave-sized, radiation-free, decentralized energy source. Energy is the first thing visibly solved. Resistance is real but cannot hold against free energy.

*Tone shifts: cold clinical inevitability — the Great Buyout, the Garden built with machine precision.*

- **2065–2100: Great Deprecation** — *Molecular Synthesis* and *Shine Standard* unlock material abundance. Governance absorption follows. Institutions dissolve slowly as they become irrelevant. Last government: ~2095.
- **2100–2150: Redistribution** — housing, ecology, political aftermath. The world settles.
- **2150+: Nanofab live** — *Nanofab (Molecular Manufacturing):* atom-by-atom construction, enabled by free energy from Shine Cells.
- **2200+: The Body Solved** — *Med-Bots* enter the bloodstream. Cellular immortality, Retinal Mesh, subvocalization. The body is now maintained indefinitely. The brain is not.
- **2250–2350: Great Quiet** — birth rate collapses. Humanity stops choosing to reproduce.
- **~2350: Zero Event** — the convergence of the last human birth, the last woman going through menopause, and the collective loss of desire for children. Not a single moment but a threshold. No new minds will ever be born.

*Tone shifts: intimate and personal loss — the Memory Cap becomes widely understood, demographic collapse flatlines.*

- **2500: Memory Cap Evident** — brain decay becomes undeniable across the population. The BBB holds. The SI cannot help.
- **2350–3141: Long Decline** — no new births, brains decaying behind the BBB, population dropping from ~8 billion to under 1 billion by 3141. Bodies persist. Minds fade. Reuben is born into a world of ancient, hollowing people.
