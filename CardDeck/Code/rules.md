## Layout (per card)
- Rank label: top-left and bottom-right corners
- Suit symbol: center
- Suit symbol (small): next to corner rank labels
- Face cards (Jack, Queen, King): placeholder block for illustration
- Card border: 12px rounded rectangle, suit color
- Triangle cards: border drawn with RGB gradient edges, black fill center
