# ONE: El Fisico's Arc

---

The alarm clock went off, but it wasn't my phone ringing. Lying next to me was a beautiful local girl I had met two nights before at the local club. I looked at the time and realized it was already 9:20. I had promised the posada keep I would not show up for breakfast last minute, and so I got up from my treehouse shack and descended the rustic stairs into the central garden, proceeding towards the lush reception where the breakfast was being prepared.

I had been staying there for just a couple of days, but it already felt like home. I ordered two breakfasts, and still half asleep, my mind started wandering off towards frequencies, rhythms, vibrations — a common thinking topic I was dedicating more and more of my free "thinking" time to during these tropical vacations, away from my day job. Back home, I sat in a tall office building in central Tel Aviv, in front of multiple monitors, developing the next generation of performance optimized trading systems that would usher the HFT firm I worked at into the future.
